  • BITS Pilani Students Discuss Augmented Reality in Education on DD National

    BITS Pilani Students Discuss Augmented Reality in Education on DD National

    Two students of BITS, Pilani, Pilani Campus Ahmad Faraaz and Sashakt Tripathi have recently appeared on DD National to discuss about the technology-enabled academic benefits of their BITSian startup venture Elixar Systems, Rajasthan. In this connection, they took part in Live Interactive session on “Augmented Reality in Education” discussing with the anchor of program about the working of three-dimensional or augmented reality in education and in general the increasing beneficial role of technology in education. They also responded to online queries of school-going students in regard to the working of augmented reality in better understanding the content of their text books especially the details of science experiments that are printed in their text books. 
     
    The recording of live interactive session is available on the following website link:
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AvOWun2NtEQ&feature=youtu.be
     

  • Divij Sharan Wins Gold Medal in Asian Games

    Divij Sharan Wins Gold Medal in Asian Games

    It is great news and a proud moment for Bitsians to hear that "Divij Sharan" son of Mr. Madhav Sharan has done us proud as he has won the gold medal in Tennis Doubles while playing with Rohan Bopanna in the Asian Games that are being held in Jakarta, Indonesia during August 18, 2018 to September 2, 2018.  Madhav Sharan is from 1972 Batch and also used to play Tabla and was connected with the Music Club and had won several awards in Oasis.

    BITS Pilani students bag international scholarships
